# Course A within SFI

Course A is the first course within **Study Path 1** and is adapted for participants with minimal or no formal schooling.

## Target Group

Course A is for you if you:

- Are illiterate or have very limited reading/writing skills
- Have 0-4 years of schooling from your home country
- Need to learn the basics of reading and writing
- Need to develop basic oral communication skills

## Learning Objectives

After completing Course A, you should be able to:

### Reading and Writing
- Recognize and write the letters of the Swedish alphabet
- Read simple words and short sentences
- Write your name and personal information
- Fill in simple forms

### Oral Communication
- Greet others and introduce yourself
- Ask and answer simple questions
- Understand common everyday expressions
- Participate in simple conversations about familiar topics

### Listening
- Understand simple instructions
- Follow everyday conversations at a slow pace
- Recognize common Swedish sounds and words

## Content Areas

| Area | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Alphabet** | Letter recognition, phonics, pronunciation |
| **Basic Vocabulary** | Numbers, colors, days, months, family |
| **Everyday Situations** | Shopping, transportation, healthcare |
| **Swedish Society** | Basic customs, school system, work life |
| **Digital Skills** | Using a computer, typing, internet basics |

## Duration

- **Approximately 50 weeks** of full-time study
- Flexible pace based on individual progress
- Often combined with practical elements

## Assessment

Course A is assessed through:

- **Continuous assessment** by the teacher
- **Portfolio** of written work
- **Oral presentations**
- **Teacher's judgment** (no formal exam)

## After Course A

After completing Course A, you proceed to **Course B** within Study Path 1.
