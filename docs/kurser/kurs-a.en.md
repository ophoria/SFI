# Course A within SFI

Course A is the first course within **Study Path 1** and is aimed at persons who lack basic reading and writing skills.

## Target Group

Course A is for you who:
- Lack reading and writing skills in your mother tongue
- Have very short or no schooling
- Need to learn to read and write from scratch

## Course Goals

After completing the course, you should be able to:

### Reading and Writing
- Recognize letters and sounds
- Read simple words and sentences
- Write simple words and sentences
- Use basic writing rules

### Speaking and Listening
- Understand simple instructions and questions
- Ask and answer simple questions
- Introduce yourself
- Handle everyday situations (shopping, visiting healthcare)

### Grammar and Vocabulary
- Basic word order
- Simple sentence parts
- Common everyday words
- Numbers and basic numeracy

## Teaching Format

Course A is **primarily oral** and practical:
- No written exams
- Focus on oral communication
- Practical exercises
- Visual support materials

## Content

### Language Areas
1. **Introduction** — Saying who you are, where you come from
2. **Everyday Life** — Home, family, food
3. **Society** — Simple information about Sweden
4. **Work** — Occupations and workplaces

### Exercises
- Sound and letter training
- Rhymes and chants
- Simple dialogues
- Role-play in everyday situations

## Assessment

### Grades
- **G** — Pass
- **IG** — Fail

!!! warning "No national exams"
    For Course A, there are no national exams. Assessment is made by the teacher based on the course goals.

### Assessment Criteria
- Participation in teaching
- Oral performance
- Practical exercises
- Continuous assessment

## Course Length

Course length varies depending on:
- Prior knowledge
- Study pace (full-time or part-time)
- Individual prerequisites

Typical study time: **200–400 hours**

## Continuation

After passing Course A, you continue with:
- **Course B** (within Study Path 1)
