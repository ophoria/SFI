# SFI - Swedish for Immigrants

Welcome to the SFI Knowledge Base — a collection of information about Swedish for Immigrants (Svenska för invandrare).

## What is SFI?

**Swedish for Immigrants (SFI)** is a free-of-charge education program for adults who need to learn Swedish. The program is available to residents who:

- Are 16 years or older
- Lack basic Swedish language skills
- Are registered in a Swedish municipality

## Why Learn Swedish?

Swedish proficiency opens doors to:

- **Employment opportunities** in the Swedish labor market
- **Further education** at university and college level
- **Social integration** and participation in society
- **Citizenship** — Swedish language skills are required for citizenship
