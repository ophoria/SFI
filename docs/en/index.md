# SFI - Swedish for Immigrants

Welcome to the SFI Knowledge Base — a collection of information about Swedish for Immigrants (SFI), its structure, courses, study paths, and regulations.

## What is SFI?

**Swedish for Immigrants (SFI)** is a free municipal adult education course in Swedish for adult immigrants in Sweden. The education has been part of the public school system since 1994 and aims to provide basic knowledge of the Swedish language and Swedish society.

## Key Facts

- **Right to SFI**: Persons with residence permits registered in Sweden who don't speak Swedish
- **Age**: From the second half of the year you turn 16
- **Must start within**: Three months of registration
- **Cost**: Free for participants
- **Education obligation**: Applies to newly arrived since January 1, 2018
