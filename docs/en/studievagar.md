# Study Paths within SFI

SFI education is offered within **three study paths** (studievägar), each designed for different educational backgrounds and needs.

## Summary

| Study Path | Target Group | Courses | Duration |
|------------|-------------|---------|----------|
| **Study Path 1** | Illiterate or minimal schooling | A, B | 1-2 years |
| **Study Path 2** | 6-9 years of schooling | B, C | 1-1.5 years |
| **Study Path 3** | 10+ years / university | C, D | 0.5-1 year |

---

## Study Path 1

**For:** Participants with minimal or no formal schooling

### Target Group

- Illiterate individuals (analfabeter)
- Those with 0-4 years of schooling
- Persons needing to learn to read and write

### Structure

| Course | Focus | Duration |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Course A** | Basic literacy, oral Swedish | ~50 weeks |
| **Course B** | Continued literacy, basic written Swedish | ~50 weeks |

### Content Focus

- **Alphabet and phonics**
- **Basic reading and writing**
- **Oral communication**
- **Everyday situations**
- **Digital skills** (basic computer use)

---

## Study Path 2

**For:** Participants with 6-9 years of schooling

### Target Group

- Those with elementary school background
- Persons who can read and write in their native language
- Learners ready for structured language study

### Structure

| Course | Focus | Duration |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Course B** | Basic Swedish (spoken and written) | ~35 weeks |
| **Course C** | Intermediate Swedish | ~35 weeks |

### Content Focus

- **Grammar and vocabulary**
- **Reading comprehension**
- **Written production**
- **Listening skills**
- **Swedish society and culture**

---

## Study Path 3

**For:** Participants with 10+ years of schooling or university education

### Target Group

- Highly educated individuals
- University graduates
- Those with strong study skills
- Learners progressing quickly

### Structure

| Course | Focus | Duration |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Course C** | Intermediate-advanced Swedish | ~25 weeks |
| **Course D** | Advanced Swedish (secondary school level) | ~25 weeks |

### Content Focus

- **Academic Swedish**
- **Complex texts**
- **Essay writing**
- **Professional communication**
- **Advanced grammar**

---

## Placement Test

Before starting SFI, you take a **placement test** that determines:

1. **Your study path** (1, 2, or 3)
2. **Starting course** (A, B, C, or D)
3. **Suitable learning pace**

### What the Test Covers

- Reading comprehension
- Writing ability
- Oral skills
- Listening comprehension

---

## Changing Study Paths

You can **change study paths** during your SFI education if:

- Your learning pace differs from expectations
- You need more/less time than initially estimated
- Your teacher recommends a change
