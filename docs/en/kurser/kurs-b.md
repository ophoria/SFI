# Course B within SFI

Course B is the second course within **Study Path 1** and the first course within **Study Path 2**.

## Target Group

Course B is for you who:
- Have completed Course A (Study Path 1)
- Have basic reading and writing skills
- Have shorter schooling (Study Path 2)

## Course Goals

After completing the course, you should be able to:

### Reading
- Read and understand simple texts
- Find specific information in short texts
- Understand common words and phrases in everyday contexts

### Writing
- Write simple messages and shorter texts
- Fill in forms
- Write simple letters and emails
- Use basic writing rules

### Speaking
- Participate in simple conversations
- Describe everyday activities
- Give and ask for information
- Present topics in a simple way

### Listening
- Understand the main content in clear speech
- Understand simple instructions
- Follow short conversations

## Teaching Format

Course B combines oral and written teaching:
- Both speaking and writing exercises
- Reading and listening comprehension
- Practical exercises
- Work with authentic texts

## Content

### Themes
1. **Everyday Life** — Home, family, leisure
2. **Society** — School, healthcare, authorities
3. **Working Life** — Occupations, workplace, job seeking
4. **Communication** — Phone, email, social media

### Language Areas
- **Vocabulary**: Common everyday words and phrases
- **Grammar**: Basic sentence structure, tenses (present, past)
- **Pronunciation**: Basic pronunciation and stress

## National Exams

!!! info "National exam exists"
    For Course B, there are national exams issued by the Swedish National Agency for Education.

### Parts of the Exam
- Reading comprehension
- Writing skills
- Listening comprehension
- Oral presentation

## Assessment

### Grades
- **G** — Pass
- **IG** — Fail

The grade is based on:
- National exam (if applicable)
- Continuous assessment
- Course goals

## Course Length

Typical study time: **200–400 hours**

Varies depending on:
- Study pace
- Prior knowledge
- Individual prerequisites

## Continuation

After passing Course B:
- **Study Path 1**: Completed education
- **Study Path 2**: Continuation to **Course C**
