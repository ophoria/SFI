# Course D within SFI

Course D is the final course within **Study Path 3** and corresponds to **Swedish as a Second Language at secondary school level**.

## Target Group

Course D is for you if you:

- Have completed Course C (within Study Path 3)
- Have strong academic background
- Aim for higher education or professional positions
- Need advanced Swedish proficiency

## Learning Objectives

After completing Course D, you should be able to:

### Reading
- Read complex texts (literature, academic articles, reports)
- Analyze and critically evaluate texts
- Understand nuanced and abstract content
- Read extensively for work and study

### Writing
- Write well-structured essays and reports
- Use advanced grammar and vocabulary
- Adapt writing style to different contexts
- Express complex arguments and analyses

### Speaking
- Participate fluently in discussions and debates
- Give professional presentations
- Express nuanced opinions and hypotheses
- Use Swedish confidently in all situations

### Listening
- Understand extended speech on complex topics
- Follow academic lectures and professional presentations
- Understand various dialects and registers
- Process information from multiple sources

## Content Areas

| Area | Content |
|------|---------|
| **Advanced Grammar** | All verb forms, complex clause structures |
| **Academic Vocabulary** | University-level terminology |
| **Literature** | Swedish fiction and non-fiction |
| **Academic Skills** | Essay writing, source criticism, argumentation |
| **Professional Swedish** | Workplace communication, formal writing |
| **Media & Society** | Current events, media analysis |

## Duration

- **Approximately 25 weeks** of full-time study
- Intensive pace with high expectations

## Assessment

Course D has **formal assessment** including:

- **Final written exam** — essay writing and text analysis
- **Final oral exam** — presentation and discussion
- **Continuous assessment** throughout the course
- **Portfolio** of written work

## National Tests

Students in Course D may take **national tests** (nationella prov) that:

- Assess proficiency against national standards
- Provide documentation for employers/educators
- Are developed by the Swedish National Agency for Education

## After Completing Course D

Course D corresponds to **Grade E in Swedish as a Second Language at secondary school level**. After SFI, you can continue with:

### Swedish Studies
- **SAS Grund** (Basic secondary level)
- **SAS 1-3** (Upper secondary level)
- **Swedish for Academics**

### Education
- **Upper secondary school** (Komvux)
- **University foundation year**
- **Vocational training programs**

### Employment
Course D prepares you for jobs requiring:
- Fluent Swedish
- Written communication
- Professional interaction
